Biography

I am a senior undergraduate student at Sun Yat-sen University, majoring in Intelligent Science and Technology. My research interests lie in Computer Vision and Generative Models, specifically focusing on Diffusion Models and Open-Vocabulary Object Detection.

Currently, I am working on improving the inference efficiency of diffusion transformers and exploring their applications in embodied AI agents. I plan to pursue a Ph.D. degree starting in Fall 2026.
